Instruction: lda/ldb/ldc/ldd

lda [location]
ldb [location]
ldc [location]
ldd [location]
0111Rd (2 bits)Location (6 bits)

Action

Takes two operands: a destination register Rd, and a location.
The destination register must be one of A, B, C, or D.
The value at the specified location is loaded into the register Rd.

Unlike with most instructions, load and store instructions do not have a conditional counterpart. However, cflg can be used to conditionally execute this instruction.

Flags

  • All processor flags are unaffected.
