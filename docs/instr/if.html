Instruction: ifx

ifx [condition]
011011Condition (6 bits)

Action

The if* instructions take in a 6-bit condition, which is interpreted as described in the Conditions section, and modify the P flag based on whether that condition is true or false.
Almost all instructions can be made to execute conditionally based on the value of the P flag.

if directly sets the P flag's value to the specified condition. (P := condition)
ifa performs a logical AND of the P flag and the condition. (P := P AND condition)
ifo performs a logical OR of the P flag and the condition. (P := P OR condition)
ifx performs a logical XOR of the P flag and the condition. (P := P XOR condition)

Flags

  • Z: Unchanged.
  • S: Unchanged.
  • K: Unchanged.
  • V: Unchanged.
  • P: Modified based on the specified condition.
